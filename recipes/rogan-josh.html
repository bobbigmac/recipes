Rogan Josh

Ingredients

  • 700g lamb shoulder, cubed
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 2 tbsp tomato purée
  • 200g Greek yogurt
  • 2 tsp ground coriander
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Brown lamb in oil, set aside.
  2. Fry onions until golden, add garlic and ginger.
  3. Add all spices, cook 1 min.
  4. Stir in tomato purée, cook 2 min.
  5. Return lamb, add yogurt, mix well.
  6. Add 200ml water, simmer covered 1.5 hours until tender.
  7. Uncover, reduce sauce, season to taste.
