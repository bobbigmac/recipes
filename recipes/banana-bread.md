# Banana Bread


    ## Ingredients
    - 3 ripe bananas, mashed  
    - ½ cup melted butter  
    - 1 cup sugar  
    - 1 egg  
    - 1 tsp vanilla  
    - 1 tsp baking soda  
    - 1 ½ cups flour  

    ## Instructions
    1. Mix wet ingredients.  
    2. Fold in dry; pour into loaf tin.  
    3. Bake 175 °C 55‑60 min.
