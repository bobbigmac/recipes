# Fried Rice


    ## Ingredients
    - 2 cups cooked rice (cold)  
    - 1 tbsp oil  
    - 1 egg, beaten  
    - 1 cup mixed veggies  
    - 2 tbsp soy sauce  

    ## Instructions
    1. Scramble egg; set aside.  
    2. Stir‑fry veggies; add rice, soy sauce, egg.  
    3. Heat through, serve hot.
