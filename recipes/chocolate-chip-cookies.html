Chocolate Chip Cookies

## Ingredients
- 1 cup butter, softened  
- 1 cup sugar  
- 1 cup brown sugar  
- 2 eggs  
- 3 cups flour  
- 1 tsp baking soda  
- 2 cups chocolate chips  

## Instructions
1. Cream butter & sugars; beat in eggs.  
2. Stir in dry ingredients; fold chips.  
3. Scoop onto tray; bake 180 °C 10‑12 min.
