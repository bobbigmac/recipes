Tomato Soup

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800 g canned tomatoes
  • 500 ml vegetable stock
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion & garlic.
  2. Add tomatoes & stock; simmer 15 min.
  3. Blend smooth; season.
