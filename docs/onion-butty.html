Onion Butty

Ingredients

  • 1/2 large white onion, sliced
  • Plenty of salt
  • Plenty of black pepper
  • 2 slices of bread
  • Garlic mayo

Instructions

  1. Slice the onion thinly and put it in a bowl.
  2. Add plenty of salt and pepper, then mix well.
  3. Let it sit for a few minutes so the salt draws out some moisture.
  4. Spread garlic mayo on one or both slices of bread.
  5. Pile the seasoned onions between the bread slices.
  6. Eat. Better than beating them away with a stick.
