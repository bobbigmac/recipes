French Toast

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 egg
  • ¼ cup milk
  • Pinch cinnamon
  • Butter for frying

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg, milk, cinnamon.
  2. Dip bread; fry both sides in butter until golden.
