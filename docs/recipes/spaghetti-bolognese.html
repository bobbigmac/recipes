Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • 400 g spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500 g minced beef
  • 400 g canned tomatoes
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic in oil.
  2. Brown beef, season.
  3. Add tomatoes; simmer 20 min.
  4. Cook pasta; toss with sauce.
