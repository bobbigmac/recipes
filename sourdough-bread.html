Sourdough Bread

Ingredients

  • 500g strong bread flour (or GF flour blend + 1 tsp xanthan gum)
  • 350ml water
  • 100g active sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour and water, let rest 30 min (autolyse).
  2. Add starter and salt, knead 10 min.
  3. Stretch and fold every 30 min for 3 hours.
  4. Shape and place in banneton.
  5. Cold proof 12-18 hours in fridge.
  6. Bake 250°C 20 min with steam, then 220°C 25 min.
